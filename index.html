Nicholas D'Angelo

Introduction

My name is Nicholas D'Angelo. I am an artist experimenting with speed, motion, and narrative. My art is personal and emotional. I create compositions driven by action, speed of movement, and thought. I document dynamic performances using various techniques to create source material for much of my work. The completed work is composed of a narrative that depicts motion in two-dimensional images.
